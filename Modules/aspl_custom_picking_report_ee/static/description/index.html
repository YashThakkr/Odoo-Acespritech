Odoo 17 Custom Picking Report (Enterprise)

This module allows to Show the Total of ordered and delivered quantity of products in report and do the signture or write their name on the pdf.

Note: This module is tested and working in Odoo Enterprise edition but not tested in Community version. And we are supporting Ubuntu OS and not Windows OS.

Features

  • Show Total of ordered and delivered quantity of products in Delivery Slip and do the signature and write his/her name on the pdf.
  • Show Total quantity of products in Picking Operations report and do the signature or write their name on the pdf.

Available for Community

How to use

  • Go to Delivery order.


  • Print the Picking Operation report from delivery order and its show total quantity of product in report and do the signature or write their name on the pdf.




  • Print the Delivery Slip from delivery order and its show the sum of ordered and delivered quantity product in report and do the signature or write their name on the pdf.
