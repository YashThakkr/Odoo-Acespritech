Odoo 15 BluePay Payment Gateway Service (Community)

BluePay Payment Gateway Service Features for Community Version

Note: This module is tested and working in Odoo community edition but not tested in Enterprise version. And we are supporting Ubuntu OS and not Windows OS.


Features

  • Payment Processing: A single provider for all your payment needs. Your customer can pay anywhere, anytime, and through multiple channels
  • International Processing: Unlock new markets and benefit from the rapid growth of international sales
  • Fraud & Risk Management: Reduce the costs of fraud to your business whilst ensuring that genuine customers are not rejected or left frustrated

How to use

  • Website > Configuration > Payment Acquirers
  • From the list of payment Acquire select BluePay


  • Bluepay Credentials: Take Account ID, Bluepay Secret Key and Account Name and put into bluepay Payment Acquire in Odoo.


  • Company currency and API currency should be same
  • Here need to configure Journal


  • If you want to generate invoice at the time of online payment then you have to configure this settings


  • Now let's do payment from website so open website and make order
  • Select bluepay from payment methods.


  • You need to follow this payment step and fill all required details






  • User can see confirm order detail by click on return option and it will show this


  • Transaction Record has been created in Payment Transactions


  • Here you can check sale order and invoice inside transactions


  • Here you can check sale order and invoice in details
